### 4.4 Source-level usage evidence

crates are used **pervasively**, not in a single optional place:

- `native/src/main.rs:31-53` ŌĆö `pheno_errors::AppError` for the canonical error

bootstrap, and the env-flag loader ŌĆö well outside the scope of an "absorb" operation
and well outside the scope of an automated agent task.
