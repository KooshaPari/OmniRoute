- Identical tip SHA is exact evidence of duplicate branch tips.
- Normalized name families are candidates only, not proof of patch equivalence.
- The exhaustive patch-ID and touched-subsystem pass was stopped at owner request. Unknown or preliminary fields are marked accordingly.
- Salvage means reconstruct or cherry-pick verified commits onto a fresh branch from main; never merge donor history wholesale.
