**Result:** âťŚ **FAILED**

- `syn` v2.0.117 â€” compilation failure

- `system-configuration` v0.7.0 â€” compilation failure

**Diagnosis:** These are **transitive dependency crates** (`rustix`, `syn`, `cc`, `system-configuration`), not the project's own crates. The failures are likely caused by one of the following:
1. **Corrupted/stale incremental build artifacts** in `target/debug/` â€” the `target` directory contains partial artifacts from previous builds.
2. **Concurrent `cargo` lock** â€” a previous `cargo` process may have timed out and left a file lock in `.cargo` or `target/`.
3. **Compiler version mismatch** â€” `rustc 1.95.0` (Homebrew) may produce incompatible metadata with artifacts built by a slightly different `stable` toolchain.

**Recommended fix:** `cargo clean` followed by a fresh `cargo check` with a single Cargo job (`cargo check --all-targets -j1`) to reduce concurrency pressure.
